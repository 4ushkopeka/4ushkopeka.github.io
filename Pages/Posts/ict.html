Why Google?

Primary Task/Core Business: Google's core business centers around delivering internet-related services and products, including its search engine, online advertising, cloud computing, software applications (Android, Chrome), and hardware products (Pixel, Google Nest).
Different Functions/Roles: Google offers a wide array of job functions and roles, ranging from software engineering and product management to marketing, data science, UX design, and more.
Specific Technologies/Services: Google engages with various technologies and services, such as Google Search, Google Ads, Google Cloud Platform, Android, Chrome, Google Assistant, TensorFlow, Pixel smartphones, Google Nest devices, web technologies, machine learning, big data, maps (Google Maps), and video sharing (YouTube).
Company Culture: Google's culture is known for its informality, innovation, and user-centric focus, fostering a creative, collaborative, and experimental work environment.
References: Information about Google's core business can be found on their official About page. Details on roles are available on the Google Careers website. Specific technologies and services are outlined on the Google Products page.

Personal Perspective: I find Google an enticing place to work due to its commitment to innovation and global impact. The company's focus on user-centric products and involvement in cutting-edge tech align with my interests. Google's culture of creativity and collaboration, along with the diverse range of roles, offers an opportunity to contribute to transformative projects and shape the future of tech.

Why Microsoft?

Primary Task/Core Business: Microsoft's core business centers around providing software, hardware, and cloud services. They develop and sell operating systems (Windows), productivity software (Microsoft Office), cloud computing solutions (Azure), and hardware products (Surface devices and Xbox).
Different Functions/Roles: Microsoft offers a wide array of job functions and roles, including software development, product management, marketing, sales, data science, UX design, hardware engineering, cybersecurity, and more, detailed on the Microsoft Careers website.
Specific Technologies/Services: Microsoft is associated with numerous technologies and services, such as Windows, Microsoft Office, Azure, Visual Studio, Surface, Xbox, Azure AI, SQL Server, and Dynamics 365.
Company Culture: Microsoft's company culture is characterized by professionalism, innovation, and a commitment to ethical AI development. It promotes a growth mindset, diversity and inclusion, and work-life balance, fostering creativity and collaboration.
References: Information about Microsoft's primary business and products can be found on their About page. Details on roles and careers are available on the Microsoft Careers website.

Personal Perspective: Microsoft is an appealing workplace due to its global influence and commitment to innovation. Their focus on AI and cloud computing aligns with my interest in emerging technologies. Ethical AI development and diversity and inclusion initiatives resonate with my values. The opportunity to work on widely-used software and hardware products is enticing, and Microsoft's culture of collaboration and growth creates an ideal work environment.

Why Oracle?

Primary Task/Core Business: Oracle's core business centers around providing database software, cloud computing, and enterprise software solutions. They specialize in database management systems (Oracle Database), cloud infrastructure (Oracle Cloud), and various software applications for businesses.
Different Functions/Roles: Oracle offers a wide array of job functions and roles, including database administration, software development, cloud architecture, sales, consulting, data science, and more, detailed on the Oracle Careers website.
Specific Technologies/Services: Oracle is associated with various technologies and services, including Oracle Database, Oracle Cloud (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS), and enterprise software for CRM, ERP, SCM, and more.
Company Culture: Oracle's company culture is often characterized as professional and business-focused, prioritizing innovation and customer success. It is results-oriented and emphasizes performance and achievement.
References: Information about Oracle's primary business and products can be found on their About Oracle page. Details on roles and careers are available on the Oracle Careers website.

Personal Perspective: Working at Oracle appeals to those interested in database technology and enterprise solutions, aligning with a passion for tech innovation. Oracle's professional, results-oriented culture offers a challenging and rewarding work environment. The opportunity to contribute to critical business software and cloud solutions is a compelling aspect of joining Oracle.
