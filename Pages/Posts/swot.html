SWOT analysis

What are they?

SWOT analysis

  • Strengths
  • Weaknesses
  • Opportunities
  • Threats
  • Combat strategies

Author: Georgi Chitarliev

Posted on: 13.09.2023

Location: Middelburg

So... What are they?

Strengths:

Logic ramble ahead... It is perhaps the greatest strength I possess. Being able to use it practically, in a program/task, for example, as back-up in arguments and in studying. This is why I struggle in classes devoid of logic (Arts, language learning etc) and cannot understand people who choose not to use it (certain groups of people pop-up immediately...). Another strength that comes to mind is determination. The amount of times I have devoted to solving a math problem/programming task are countless. Sometimes I go as far as ignoring bedtimes (sometimes till 5am!) and other human needs, like food. Those qualities definetely, in my opinion at least, are going to come in handy in my current study! But not everything is perfect...

weaknesses:

When it comes to weaknesses, most of them are personal traits that can be overcome, although I do fail at times. The first thing is the sweet tooth. Not only does it contribute to weight gain, but also to selfishness. Another one is the procrastination. Usually it does not last, as my determination kicks in, but sometimes leads to huge loss of time, which is often pivotal. And lastly, lack of physical exercise. Despite enjoying sports, I find it hard to incorporate physical activity in my life, albeit I do take the opportunities when they do present themselves. Which leads to...

Opportunities:

Apart from occasionally indulging in hobbies as a means of procrastination, there are seldom any significant chances available here. Even when such opportunities do arise, they typically revolve around gaming or socializing, with limited options for meeting new people. The situation is quite similar when it comes to satisfying one's sweet tooth. However, sports emerge as a notable prospect, offering not only a solution to combat a sedentary lifestyle but also a means of connecting with others and unleashing one's competitive spirit. Furthermore, logic and determination serve as valuable assets that can be applied effectively across various areas of life. Nevertheless, where there are positives, there are also downsides to consider.

Threats:

The biggest threat to my own study is, unironically, me. The only thing capable of spreading chaos and disorganisation - my own mind. My mental health has suffered immensely from a stupid brain malfunction, commonly known as OCD. Thankfully, a solution has been found as is currently being enforced, so hopefully this threat is gone for good. But what about the weaknesses?

Combat Strategies:

The elephant in the room has been addressed: OCD, and the solution. But what about my other weaknesses? The sweet thooth can easily be shut down, as far as you are self-disciplined and limit your sugar intake. Procrastination can be dealt with in a simillar way: giving yourself a small amount of time to deescalate the pressure in your head. Just make sure to get back to work.

I also have a sweet tooth! I got it from the FAZ stage at the pizzeria!

We all know there is not a single problem the Nyan cat cannot solve so pass by sometime!

I'm gonna reach down OCD's throat, and turn it's lungs into mittens!

Sometimes you should search for the peace of your mind, instead of for facts...
